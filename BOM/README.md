# Sourcing

## Why Amazon?
I am well aware that Hack Club prefers sourcing electronics from less mainstream distributors than Amazon. However, for my case, I ended up saving about 20 dollars and weeks worth of waiting through using Amazon with my parent's Prime membership (free shipping and heavily reduced fees). Because I bought so few electronics, and the ones I did buy were heavy/large, shipping from Aliexpress would have costed upwards of 40 dollars, not including myriad other taxes and fees. Even if I did save some money from buying a cheaper part, the shipping would have quickly covered that cost.
## Why McMaster-Carr?
McMaster-Carr is a local company that I frequently rely on for robotics parts. They have some of the best prices for highly specified materials, and because they are very local, shipping is fast and very cheap. They offer a fixed shipping fee for as many items as you want, making bulk/bundled purchases ideal.

## Self Sourcing
You will notice that the BOM does not include the 2020 extrusions, standoffs, bolts, VESC, PSU, etc. This is because I have self-sourced these parts from friends, robotics, or previous personal projects.
